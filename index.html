Dr. Emily Carter

Registered Dental Hygienist

Specializing in pediatric and geriatric dental care

About Dr. Carter

Dr. Emily Carter is a dedicated and compassionate dental hygienist with over 10 years of experience. She earned her degree from the University of Dental Sciences and is committed to providing gentle and effective care to patients of all ages. Dr. Carter believes in a holistic approach to oral health, focusing on prevention and patient education to achieve long-term wellness. Her friendly demeanor and personalized approach help patients feel at ease, ensuring a comfortable and positive experience.

Philosophy

Dr. Carter's philosophy centers on building trusting relationships with her patients. She takes the time to understand each individual's unique needs and concerns, tailoring treatment plans accordingly. Her goal is to empower patients to take control of their oral health through education and proactive care. Dr. Carter stays up-to-date with the latest advancements in dental hygiene to provide the highest standard of care.

Specializations

Dr. Carter has a special interest in pediatric and geriatric dental care. She understands the unique challenges and considerations involved in treating children and seniors, and she is adept at creating a comfortable and supportive environment for these patients. Her expertise includes preventive care, gentle cleanings, and addressing age-related dental issues with sensitivity and skill.
